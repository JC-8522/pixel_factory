# Prompt 01: Floor Scene

## Purpose

Generate the base office room art direction.

## Prompt

Create a cozy pixel-art office room in a Stardew Valley-inspired style, viewed from a straight-on top-down management-game angle. The room should feel handcrafted and warm, with a dark wooden outer frame, cream wall, blue-gray tiled carpet floor, and decorative office props around the edges. Include a pantry or coffee station in the top-left, a double door on the top wall, a blue sofa and small table near the top-right, a bookshelf, a printer or utility machine on the right wall, and plant decorations along the bottom edge. Leave the center floor area clean and clearly reserved for twelve workstation slots in a 4 by 3 layout. Use crisp pixel art, strong outlines, warm wood tones, muted blue-gray flooring, and no modern glossy UI styling.

## Output Intent

- base room shell
- art direction reference
- empty office preview
